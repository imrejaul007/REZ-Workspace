```
WRONG PRICING:
├── ₹0 (free) → no skin in game, no value perception
├── ₹9999/mo → too expensive for SMB trust
└── Complex tiers → confusing for first-time buyers

RIGHT PRICING:
├── ₹299/mo → cheap enough to try, expensive enough to value
├── Monthly billing → no commitment fear
├── First month free → lower barrier
```
