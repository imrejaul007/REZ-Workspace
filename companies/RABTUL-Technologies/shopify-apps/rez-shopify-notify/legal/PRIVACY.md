# Privacy Policy

**ReZ WhatsApp Notify**

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

RABTUL Technologies Private Limited operates ReZ WhatsApp Notify. This policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Shopify store and customer phone numbers
- Message content and delivery status
- Customer interaction data
- Campaign performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Deliver order and marketing notifications
- Track message delivery and engagement
- Generate analytics reports
- Improve notification delivery

## 4. WhatsApp Data Processing

- Messages processed through WhatsApp Business API
- Phone numbers stored for notification delivery
- Message metadata retained for analytics

## 5. Data Sharing

- WhatsApp Business API for message delivery
- Analytics services for reporting
- Legal authorities when required

## 6. Data Retention

- Message logs: 90 days
- Campaign data: 12 months
- Deleted within 30 days of uninstallation

## 7. Security

End-to-end encryption for message content. TLS for data in transit.

Request access, correction, or deletion of your data: privacy@rez.money
