# Privacy Policy

**ReZ Cart Recover**

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

RABTUL Technologies Private Limited ("we", "our", or "us") operates ReZ Cart Recover. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Shopify store URL and authentication tokens
- Contact information for account setup
- Configuration preferences

### Information Collected Automatically
- Cart abandonment events and recovery actions
- Notification delivery status
- API usage and error logs

### Information from Third Parties
- Shopify order and customer data (with your authorization)
- Notification delivery confirmations from third-party providers

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the cart recovery service
- Send abandoned cart notifications via configured channels
- Generate analytics and reports on recovery performance
- Improve our services and develop new features
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:
- Third-party notification providers (SMS, Email, WhatsApp) as necessary to deliver notifications
- Shopify as required by their API terms
- Service providers who assist in our operations (hosting, analytics)
- Legal authorities when required by law

## 5. Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Transaction logs are retained for 90 days
- Analytics data is anonymized after 12 months
- Upon uninstallation, data is deleted within 30 days

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Secure cloud infrastructure

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## 8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication and session management
- Service functionality
- Analytics (anonymized)

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 10. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## 11. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email.
