# Privacy Policy

**ReZ SocialSell**

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

RABTUL Technologies Private Limited operates ReZ SocialSell. This policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Customer messages and conversation history
- Social media account connections
- Support ticket and escalation data
- AI response training data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Process and respond to customer inquiries
- Train and improve AI responses
- Generate analytics on customer service performance
- Facilitate human escalation when needed

## 4. AI Data Processing

- Messages analyzed to generate AI responses
- Conversation data used for AI training (anonymized)
- Personal data protected during AI processing

## 5. Social Media Data

- Accessed only with user authorization
- Processed per platform privacy policies
- Stored securely after authorization revoked

## 6. Data Sharing

- Social platforms (as authorized)
- Human agents (for escalation)
- Analytics providers (anonymized)

## 7. Data Retention

- Conversation history: 12 months
- Analytics data: 24 months
- Deleted within 30 days of uninstallation

## 8. Security

Encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.
