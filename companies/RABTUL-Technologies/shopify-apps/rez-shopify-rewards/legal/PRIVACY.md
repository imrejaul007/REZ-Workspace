# Privacy Policy

**ReZ Loyalty Boost**

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

RABTUL Technologies Private Limited operates ReZ Loyalty Boost. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Shopify store authentication and configuration
- Customer loyalty program participation data
- Points, rewards, and tier information
- Referral tracking data
- Analytics on program engagement

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Manage loyalty points and rewards
- Calculate tier upgrades and benefits
- Generate referral credits
- Provide program analytics
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:
- Shopify (as required by API terms)
- Service providers for notification delivery
- Analytics platforms (anonymized)

## 5. Data Retention

- Loyalty records retained while account is active
- Transaction history retained for 24 months
- Deleted within 30 days of uninstallation

## 6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

## 7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your loyalty data. Contact: privacy@rez.money
