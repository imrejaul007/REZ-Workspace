## What is QR Cloud?

QR Cloud is a complete QR commerce platform that lets merchants:

- **Generate QR codes** for menus, payments, and ordering
- **Track scans** and customer behavior
- **Accept orders** directly from QR scans
- **Analyze performance** with real-time dashboards
