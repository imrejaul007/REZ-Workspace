## OVERVIEW

CARECODE is an AI-powered Healthcare Operating System that provides:
- AI Receptionist (appointment booking, patient intake)
- AI Care Coordinator (follow-ups, reminders)
- AI Pharmacist (prescription verification, drug interactions)
- AI Diagnosis Assistant (symptom analysis, referrals)
- AI Health Records (EHR, digital prescriptions)
